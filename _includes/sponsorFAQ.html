How can I sponsor?

Please email mateohacks25@gmail.com to express your interest in sponsoring our hackathon. Our team will contact you to discuss specific details, answer any additional questions you may have, and provide more information about available sponsorship opportunities.

Can we offer company-related services?

Absolutely! Offering memberships or access to your company’s products or services can be a great way to incentivize participants and build relationships with potential customers. This can take the form of a free year of a premium service or a lifetime membership, for example.

Can we participate as speakers during the event?

Yes, we welcome companies to participate in hackathons as speakers and judges. As a sponsor, you can provide insights into your industry, showcase your products and services, and help to guide participants towards developing solutions that align with your company’s values and goals.

Do we need to provide prizes for the hackathon?

Providing prizes is a great way to incentivize participants to compete and to showcase your company’s support for innovation. Prizes can include anything from cash awards to product or service packages. If this is not possible for your group, we would greatly appreciate donations of any amount.
